AI-Native Scaled Agile — Training Content Structure

A practical training blueprint for teams adopting AI-native ways of working inside Scaled Agile environments (for example SAFe-based organisations).

Last updated: 5 April 2026 (AEST). This is an implementation-ready structure that can be adapted for executives, product leaders, delivery leads, and engineering teams.

AI-Native Scaled Agile SAFe-aligned Training Design

Program Outcomes

  • Build shared AI fluency across leadership, product, delivery, and engineering.
  • Embed AI into planning, prioritisation, delivery, governance, and continuous improvement.
  • Shift from “AI as a side tool” to an AI-native operating model across value streams.
  • Improve throughput, decision quality, and time-to-value while managing risk responsibly.

Training Structure (6 Modules)

1) AI-Native Foundations & Mindset

  • What “AI-native” means in enterprise delivery.
  • Core AI concepts: ML, GenAI, LLMs, RAG, and agents.
  • Responsible AI: ethics, privacy, safety, and governance guardrails.

2) Lean-Agile Leadership in the Age of AI

  • Leadership behaviours for AI-native transformation.
  • AI-informed strategy, OKRs, portfolio prioritisation, and funding signals.
  • Operating model updates for decision speed and transparency.

3) Product & Program Planning with AI

  • AI-supported discovery, problem framing, and customer insight analysis.
  • Backlog refinement and prioritisation support (WSJF/RICE augmentation).
  • PI planning support: dependency detection, forecasting, and risk surfacing.

4) Team Technical Agility + DevOps

  • AI-assisted coding, review, test generation, and defect detection.
  • AI in CI/CD: anomaly detection, release confidence, and rollback guidance.
  • Quality engineering and secure-by-design with human oversight.

5) Inspect & Adapt with AI Analytics

  • AI-assisted retrospectives and improvement opportunity mining.
  • Flow metrics, bottleneck prediction, and scenario simulations.
  • Risk sensing and early warning indicators across ART/value stream level.

6) Role-Based Application Labs

  • Executives: investment and governance decision labs.
  • Product/Delivery: planning and prioritisation workflow redesign labs.
  • Engineers: agentic coding, testing, and delivery accelerator labs.

Suggested Delivery Cadence

  • Phase 1 (2 days): Modules 1–3 (mindset, leadership, planning).
  • Phase 2 (2 days): Modules 4–6 (technical agility, metrics, role labs).
  • Follow-up (30/60/90 days): Outcomes review, coaching, and adoption health checks.

Assessment & Evidence of Adoption

  • Capability baseline and post-training uplift check.
  • Role-based practical assessment (scenario + workflow redesign output).
  • Adoption indicators: cycle time, escaped defects, planning confidence, and value delivery consistency.
