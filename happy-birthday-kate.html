Your Birthday Poem 🎀

On a Marrickville night with the lights turned low,
Beth was ready with the birthday glow.
Drew was grinning, Ryan cracked a joke,

Kate in the middle, heart of the scene,
Laughing so hard that her eyes went green.
Pints on the table, stories in the air,
The kind of warm chaos you can only have there.

Here’s to this birthday and many years more,
To pub nights, soft pizzas, and sticky pub floors.
To Beth, Drew, Ryan and James by your side —
Happy Birthday dear Kate, with a whole bar as your pride. 🥳💖
