Donald Trump

Donald John Trump (born June 14, 1946) is an American politician, media personality, and businessman who served as the 45th president of the United States from 2017 to 2021.

Before entering politics, Trump was a businessman and television personality. He inherited his family's real estate business and expanded it, primarily by building and renovating skyscrapers, hotels, casinos, and golf courses.

Key information:

  • Born: June 14, 1946 (age 77)
  • Spouses: Ivana Trump (m. 1977; div. 1992), Marla Maples (m. 1993; div. 1999), Melania Trump (m. 2005)
  • Children: Donald Jr., Ivanka, Eric, Tiffany, Barron
  • Political Party: Republican
