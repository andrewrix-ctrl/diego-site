Mac & Cheese Pairing Tips 🥂

Some extremely serious, totally scientific pairings for your bowl:

  • 🍷
    Gouda-heavy bakes love a crisp white wine or a bright non-alc spritz with citrus.
  • 🍺
    Spicy mac wants something cold and bubbly (lager, ginger beer, or a hop-free soda).
  • 📺
    Weeknight stovetop mac pairs best with comfy clothes and a low-stakes TV show.
