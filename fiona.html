Foodie Sydney – Picks Just for You, Fiona 🍽️

A little handpicked list of Sydney spots – from cute bakeries to food trucks and easy dinner options – curated by Diego so you don’t have to doom‑scroll Google Maps.

Casual Dinner & Drinks

Winghaus, Barangaroo

📍 Shop 1, Scotch Row, Hickson Road, Barangaroo · Wings, beer & good chaos

Right in Barangaroo, Winghaus is loud, fun and very “after work exhale”. Think: wings, burgers, beer towers and screens everywhere. Not fine dining, but excellent for “I want to talk and snack and not worry about being too formal.”

Barangaroo House

📍 35 Barangaroo Ave, Barangaroo · Views + varied menus

A multi‑level venue right on the water with different vibes on each floor. Great if you want something a bit nicer but still relaxed – sharing plates, good drinks, and that “oh right, Sydney is pretty” view.

Bakeries & Sweet Things

Bourke Street Bakery

📍 Multiple locations (Surry Hills, Barangaroo, etc.) · Classic Sydney bakery energy

A Sydney staple. Sausage rolls, tarts, crusty bread, really good coffee. Perfect for a grab‑and‑go snack or a quick “I need something comforting and flaky” moment between meetings.

Flour and Stone

📍 53 Riley St, Woolloomooloo · Cake, lamingtons & very good vibes

Known for their lamingtons and beautiful cakes. Cosy and a bit indulgent – ideal for a “treat yourself” afternoon tea or picking up something nice to share.

Food Trucks & Street‑Style Options

Tramsheds & Surrounds (Forest Lodge)

📍 1 Dalgal Way, Forest Lodge · Indoor market vibe, casual eats

Not strictly food trucks, but a mix of casual vendors under one roof – pizza, ramen, gelato, wine bars. Great if you’re with people who all want different things and don’t feel like arguing.

Food Truck Parks & Pop‑ups

📍 Various spots (The Rocks, Darling Harbour, pop‑up events)

Sydney regularly has rotating food trucks at The Rocks, Darling Harbour and around events. They’re perfect for low‑key evenings: grab something, wander, people‑watch. Best discovered via event listings or just embracing “let’s walk and see what’s there.”

Breakfast & Coffee

Edition Coffee Roasters

📍 Barangaroo & Haymarket · Nordic‑Japanese inspired café

Beautiful coffee and slightly fancy breakfast options with a Nordic‑Japanese twist. Great for a pre‑work catch‑up that feels a little bit special without being over the top.

Single O

📍 Surry Hills & CBD spots · Coffee‑nerd heaven

One for coffee people. Great brews, simple but solid food, and lots of energy. Ideal if you need caffeine plus somewhere to debrief the week.
